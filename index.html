Services

Teeth Whitening

Professional-grade whitening treatments using advanced technology for a brighter, more confident smile — safely and comfortably.

LuxSmile offers professional teeth whitening that delivers results far superior to over-the-counter products. Using clinically tested whitening agents and advanced LED light technology, we can brighten your smile by several shades in a single session.

What to expect: The treatment takes approximately 60–90 minutes. A protective barrier is applied to your gums before the whitening gel is placed on your teeth. Results are immediate and long-lasting with proper care.

Who is it for? Whitening is ideal for adults with healthy teeth and gums who want to reduce staining from coffee, tea, wine, or natural discolouration. We assess your suitability at the start of every appointment.

Aftercare: We recommend avoiding dark-coloured foods and beverages for 48 hours post-treatment and maintaining a consistent oral hygiene routine to prolong your results.

Scaling & Root Planing

Deep cleaning treatments focused on restoring gum health and preventing periodontal disease through thorough removal of plaque and tartar.

Scaling and root planing is a non-surgical deep cleaning procedure designed for patients with signs of gum disease, such as deep periodontal pockets, bleeding gums, or bone loss. It goes beyond a routine cleaning to address the root surfaces of your teeth.

The process: Using specialized instruments, Leili carefully removes hardened plaque (calculus) and bacteria from below the gumline, then smooths the root surfaces to discourage future buildup and help gum tissue reattach.

Is it painful? Local anesthesia is available to ensure your comfort. Most patients experience only mild sensitivity afterward, which typically resolves within a few days.

Follow-up: After scaling and root planing, we recommend more frequent maintenance visits (every 3–4 months) to monitor your gum health and prevent recurrence.

Routine Dental Cleaning

Comprehensive prophylaxis appointments to keep your smile fresh, healthy, and free from the buildup that leads to cavities and gum disease.

A routine dental cleaning at LuxSmile is a thorough, relaxing experience. Every appointment includes a full assessment of your oral health, professional scaling to remove tartar and plaque, polishing to remove surface stains, and flossing.

What's included: Periodontal assessment, scaling above and below the gumline, polishing, flossing, and a personalized oral hygiene review. We also screen for early signs of decay, gum disease, and oral cancer.

How often? Most adults benefit from a cleaning every 6 months. However, some patients — particularly those with a history of gum disease or higher cavity risk — may need visits every 3–4 months.

Why it matters: Regular cleanings prevent the buildup that brushing and flossing alone cannot fully remove, reducing your risk of cavities, gum disease, and costly dental work down the road.

Oral Hygiene Instruction

Personalized coaching on the best at-home techniques, tools, and routines tailored to your specific oral health needs and goals.

Great oral health starts at home. LuxSmile provides one-on-one coaching to ensure you have the knowledge and tools to care for your smile between appointments. This service is tailored to your unique mouth — no generic advice.

What we cover: Proper brushing technique and timing, effective flossing methods, interdental cleaning tools (such as water flossers and soft picks), tongue cleaning, and guidance on toothpaste and mouthwash selection.

For all ages: Whether you're learning healthy habits for the first time or refining your existing routine, Leili provides clear, practical guidance that fits your lifestyle and oral health history.

Special considerations: If you wear braces, have implants, bridges, or other dental work, we provide specialized instruction to keep those areas clean and healthy.

Fluoride Therapy

Targeted remineralization treatments to strengthen enamel and protect against decay — especially beneficial for those at higher risk of cavities.

Fluoride therapy is a quick, painless, and highly effective way to protect your teeth from decay. Fluoride works by remineralizing weakened enamel and making teeth more resistant to acid attacks from bacteria and food.

Who benefits most? Fluoride therapy is especially recommended for patients with a history of frequent cavities, dry mouth (xerostomia), exposed root surfaces, sensitivity, or those undergoing orthodontic treatment.

How it works: A professional-strength fluoride varnish or gel is applied directly to the tooth surfaces after your cleaning. It takes only a few minutes and is completely comfortable.

Frequency: Most patients receive fluoride therapy at every cleaning visit. Your individual risk level will guide how often it is recommended for you.

Frequently askedquestions

From your first visit to your regular check-ups, LuxSmile is here to be your trusted partner in achieving and maintaining a healthy, beautiful smile.

  • What services does LuxSmile offer?

    LuxSmile offers a full range of dental hygiene services including routine cleanings, scaling and root planing, professional teeth whitening, fluoride therapy, oral hygiene instruction, and mobile dental hygiene for clients who prefer care in their own home.

  • Do you offer mobile dental hygiene services?

    Yes! One of our most popular offerings is our mobile dental hygiene service, where Leili comes directly to your home, office, or care facility. It's perfect for busy families, seniors, or anyone who finds it difficult to visit a clinic.

  • How often should I have my teeth professionally cleaned?

    Most patients benefit from a professional cleaning every 6 months. However, depending on your gum health, risk of cavities, and other factors, Leili may recommend more frequent visits — typically every 3 to 4 months for patients with active gum disease.

  • What is the difference between a routine cleaning and deep cleaning?

    A routine cleaning (prophylaxis) removes plaque and tartar from above and just below the gumline. A deep cleaning (scaling and root planing) goes further below the gumline to treat gum disease and remove buildup from the root surfaces. Your hygienist will recommend the appropriate treatment after an assessment.

  • Is professional teeth whitening safe?

    Yes — professional whitening performed or supervised by a registered dental hygienist is safe and effective. Leili uses high-quality whitening products and takes care to protect your gums and soft tissues. She will also assess your suitability for whitening during your appointment.

  • Do you accept dental insurance?

    LuxSmile provides detailed receipts that you can submit directly to your insurance provider for reimbursement. We recommend checking your plan's coverage for dental hygiene services. Please contact us if you have any questions about billing.

  • How do I book an appointment?

    You can book an appointment easily using our online booking form on this page, by calling us at 416-994-9669, or by emailing luxsmiledentalltd@gmail.com. We'll get back to you promptly to confirm your preferred date and time.

  • What should I expect during my first visit?

    Your first visit includes a comprehensive assessment of your oral health — including a review of your medical history, a periodontal assessment, and a full cleaning. Leili will discuss her findings with you and create a personalized care plan to meet your specific needs and goals.

  • Can you accommodate patients with dental anxiety?

    Absolutely. At LuxSmile, your comfort is our top priority. Leili takes a gentle, patient-centred approach and will always go at your pace. If you experience dental anxiety, simply let us know when booking and we'll do everything we can to make your visit as relaxed as possible.

  • What areas do you serve for mobile dental hygiene?

    LuxSmile's mobile service covers Toronto and the surrounding Greater Toronto Area. If you're unsure whether we can come to your location, please reach out and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

  • Are your products and materials safe for all patients?

    Yes. LuxSmile uses high-quality, clinically tested dental products that are safe for all patients. If you have specific allergies, sensitivities, or preferences, please let us know at the time of booking and we will make every effort to accommodate your needs.
